SteadyMind Journal (“we,” “our,” or “us”) provides a journaling and mental health tracking mobile application (“App”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1) Information We Collect

  • Account Information. When you create an account via email/password, Sign in with Apple, or Google Sign-In, we collect your email address and authentication details (and, if provided by the provider, your display name).
  • User Content. Journal entries and other information you input in the App. Entries are private and tied to your account.
  • Purchases. If you subscribe to premium features, Apple provides us purchase status/receipts to enable access. We do not receive your card or full billing details.
  • Usage & Diagnostics. App usage information, performance metrics, and crash logs collected via Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to improve reliability and experience.
  • Location. We do not collect precise or coarse location data.

2) How We Use Information

  • App Functionality. Provide account access, securely store/retrieve journal entries, and enable subscriptions.
  • Analytics & Improvements. Understand feature usage to improve performance and usability.
  • Diagnostics. Identify and fix crashes, bugs, and service issues.
  • Compliance. Meet legal and platform requirements.

3) Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • Service Provider. We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, cloud data storage, analytics, and crash reporting. Firebase acts as our processor for these services.
  • No Sale of Data. We do not sell or rent your personal data, and we do not use third-party advertising SDKs.
  • Legal. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of the service.

4) Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures, including encrypted HTTPS connections, Firebase Authentication, database security rules, and access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5) Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Update. View or update account information within the App where available.
  • Deletion. Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at [YOUR CONTACT EMAIL].
  • Subscriptions. Manage your subscription in the App Store: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • Analytics Preferences. Where platform settings allow, you may limit analytics or personalized diagnostics on your device.

6) Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn that such information has been collected, we will delete it promptly.
